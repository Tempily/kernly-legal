Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 8, 2026

Kernly ("we", "us", "the app") is a German language learning application. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information — email address, display name, and interface language preference.
  • Learning data — placement test answers, task responses, scores, study path progress, vocabulary items, and error patterns.
  • Usage data — session timestamps, streak counts, XP earned, and feature interactions (anonymised).
  • Audio recordings — short voice clips submitted during speaking exercises, processed for pronunciation feedback and immediately discarded after scoring.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To personalise your learning path, task difficulty, and content recommendations.
  • To track your progress, streaks, and vocabulary review schedule.
  • To improve task quality and exercise generation.
  • To send optional reminders and streak notifications (you can disable these in Settings).

3. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI API — task generation and answer evaluation. We send task context and your answers; no personal identifiers are transmitted.
  • Telegram Login — optional authentication via Telegram. We receive your Telegram display name and user ID only.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management. RevenueCat processes purchase data; we do not store payment details.
  • Expo Push Notifications — delivery of optional study reminders.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers within the European Union. We use encrypted connections (TLS) for all data transmission. Access to production data is restricted to authorised personnel only.

5. Data Retention

We retain your learning data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data is removed within 30 days. Anonymised, aggregated statistics may be retained for product improvement.

6. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws (including GDPR), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your data.
  • Export your learning data in a portable format.
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@kernly.app.

7. Children's Privacy

Kernly is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9. Contact

For questions about this policy or your data, email support@kernly.app.
