Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple App Store or Google Play account. Open your device Settings, go to Subscriptions, find Kernly, and cancel from there. You will retain premium access until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account?

Open the app, go to Settings, scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete account". Your personal data will be removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.

The app says "Request timed out". What should I do?

This can happen when generating complex exercises. Try again -- tasks are generated on-the-fly and a retry usually succeeds. If the issue persists, check your internet connection or contact us.

Can I use Kernly offline?

Kernly requires an internet connection for AI-generated tasks and voice exercises. Cached vocabulary and progress data is available offline.

How is my speaking evaluated?

Your voice recordings are transcribed and analysed by AI for grammar, vocabulary, clarity, and pronunciation. Results include a score, corrected version, and model answer. Recordings are not stored after evaluation.

What levels does Kernly support?

Kernly covers A1 through C1. A short placement test at the start determines your level, and tasks adapt as you improve.

I found a bug or have a feature request.

Please email support@kernly.app with a description and, if possible, a screenshot. We read every message.
